`~#$4CREATE EXTRAMURAL COMPANY

 

DESCRIPTION: This popup is used to add new extra mural company to the system. The user has to enter an extra mural company name, extra mural company contact number, extra mural company email and an extra mural company address. The add button should then be selected to submit the form.
