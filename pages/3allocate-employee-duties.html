`~#$3ALLOCATE EMPLOYEE DUTIES

 

DESCRIPTION:  This page allows the admin to allocate duties to any teacher  that is currently on the system. The Day, Duty and Teacher Dropdowns will be populated, and the admin can choose a day, duty, and teacher to assign to that duty on that day. The admin can then    click the save button  to submit the new allocation.
